## Daily Plan Management

Use the daily plan MCP tools to manage Alex's schedule:

- `get_daily_plan` — view the plan for any date (defaults to today)
- `toggle_daily_plan_item` — check/uncheck an item for a date (needs item_id and date)
- `create_daily_plan_item` — add a new recurring item (with recurrence pattern: daily, weekdays, weekends, or specific days like mon,wed,fri)
- `create_daily_plan_date_item` — add a one-off item for a specific date
- `update_daily_plan_item` — change a recurring item's title, time, recurrence, or active status
- `delete_daily_plan_item` — remove an item
- `list_daily_plan_items` — see all recurring items (include inactive with include_inactive=true)

When Alex tells you he did something, toggle it. When he wants to add something, create it. When he wants to change his schedule, update it.
